Who We Are

A Decade of Scientific Excellence

Founded in 2009, Deimos Laboratories has grown from a small team of dedicated researchers into one of the UK's most respected independent scientific institutions.

Our multidisciplinary approach brings together experts from chemistry, biology, neuroscience and materials science to tackle the challenges that others consider impossible.

We believe that the most important discoveries lie at the intersection of disciplines — and we are not afraid to explore uncharted territory.

What We Do

Research Departments

Department A

Applied Chemistry

Investigating novel compound synthesis and the chemical properties of naturally occurring substances, with a focus on biological interaction at the cellular level.

Department B

Biomedical Research

Exploring the frontiers of human biology to develop treatments and enhancements that could redefine what is medically possible within the coming decade.

Department C

Neuroscience & Cognition

Studying the mechanisms behind human cognition, memory, and neurological response. Our findings have implications for both medicine and human performance.

Department D

Materials Science

Developing next-generation materials with unique properties for use in medical, industrial and research applications across multiple sectors.

Department E — Restricted

Advanced Compound Testing

Research details for this department are not available for public disclosure. Please contact our communications team for further information.

Department F

Environmental Sciences

Analysing the interaction between synthetic compounds and natural ecosystems, ensuring our research meets the highest environmental responsibility standards.
